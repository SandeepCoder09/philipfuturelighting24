About Philips

Founded in 1891 in Eindhoven, Netherlands by Gerard Philips and Frederik Philips, the company evolved from a lamp factory into a global health technology powerhouse.

In 2026, Philips focuses on the "health continuum" — spanning healthy living, clinical diagnosis, connected care, and home treatment.
