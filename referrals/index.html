How Commission Works

🎁 First Referral Activation

When your first referral purchases a product and activates daily income, you become eligible for commission earnings.

💰 Daily Commission System

Commission is calculated from your referral's daily product income, not from the product price.

📊 Example

If your Level 1 member buys a ₹399 product and earns ₹20 per day:

You earn 3% of ₹20 = ₹1 per day.

Level 2 earns 1.5% of ₹20 = ₹0.60 per day.

Level 3 earns 0.5% of ₹20 = ₹0.40 per day.
